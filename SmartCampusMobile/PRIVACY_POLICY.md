# Privacy Policy for Smart Campus

**Last Updated:** October 16, 2025

## Introduction

Smart Campus ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following personal information:
- **Name** - For user identification
- **Email Address** - For account creation and communication
- **Phone Number** - For contact purposes (optional)
- **School ID** - To associate users with their educational institution
- **Role** - To provide appropriate access (Parent, Teacher, Student, Admin)

### Usage Data
- Device information (OS version, device model)
- App usage statistics
- Crash reports and error logs
- Access timestamps

### User-Generated Content
- Messages and communications
- Photos uploaded to gallery
- Homework submissions
- Attendance records
- Fee payment information

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- **Authentication** - Verifying user identity and managing access
- **Service Delivery** - Providing app features and functionality
- **Communication** - Sending notifications and important updates
- **Analytics** - Improving app performance and user experience
- **Security** - Protecting against unauthorized access and fraud
- **Compliance** - Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

## Data Storage and Security

### AWS Infrastructure
Your data is stored securely using Amazon Web Services (AWS):
- **AWS Cognito** - User authentication and authorization
- **Amazon DynamoDB** - Encrypted data storage
- **Amazon S3** - Secure file storage for media

### Security Measures
- Data encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Data encryption at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access control and authentication
- Automatic backups
- Secure API endpoints

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share data with:
- **School Administrators** - As necessary for school operations
- **Teachers** - Student-related information for educational purposes
- **Parents** - Information about their children
- **Service Providers** - AWS for hosting and infrastructure
- **Legal Authorities** - When required by law

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** - Request a copy of your data
- **Correction** - Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion** - Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export** - Download your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out** - Disable non-essential notifications

## Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for educational institutions and may be used by minors. We:
- Require parental consent for users under 13
- Collect only necessary information from children
- Provide parents access to their children's data
- Allow parents to request deletion of children's accounts
- Comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

## Data Retention

We retain your data:
- **Active Accounts** - For the duration of account activity
- **Deleted Accounts** - 30 days for recovery, then permanently deleted
- **Legal Requirements** - As required by law or regulation
- **Backups** - Retained for 90 days maximum

## Cookies and Tracking

The app uses:
- **Authentication Tokens** - To maintain login sessions
- **Analytics** - To improve app performance (optional)
- **Crash Reporting** - To identify and fix bugs (optional)

You can disable optional tracking in app settings.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in:
- **AWS Region** - eu-north-1 (Stockholm, Sweden)
- **Backup Region** - As configured by your institution

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** - Cloud infrastructure
- **Expo** - Mobile app development and deployment
- **Firebase** - (If enabled) Push notifications

Each service has its own privacy policy which we encourage you to review.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via:
- In-app notifications
- Email notifications
- App store update notes

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
